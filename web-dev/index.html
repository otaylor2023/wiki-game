  • Going to Central Park

But once you're at these places, what do you do? This website hopes to give you some concrete ideas to make your trip great!

Things to do in NYC

The best activities in the city have three main parts:
  1. Teach you about New York life and culture
  2. Involve more than just sightseeing
  3. Are fun!
So with that in mind, here are some of the best things to do in the city:
Activity Place Details
Bowling at Chelsea Pier Chelsea Pier Head to Hudson Yards and walk along the water on the way to Chelsea Pier, which has tons of food and fun things to do! Go bowling with your friends or family, then check out the rest of the area!
Go roller-blade dancing at Central Park! Central Park, northeast of Sheep Meadow On weekends, visit Central Park, rent roller blades, and start dancing!
